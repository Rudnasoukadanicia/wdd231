Website plan

Site Name :The Delice Resto

I chose the name The Delice Resto because it evokes gastronomy and culinary quality that other restaurants offer.

Site Objective

The Delice Resto website aims to showcase the restaurant's various culinary dishes, highlight its culinary identity, and inform visitors about the services offered, such as the menu, and will facilitate contact.

Site Scenarios

  • How can I reserve a table?
  • What are the flagship culinary dishes?
